About Me

I am a Senior Research Scientist at CIBC in Toronto, Canada. My colleagues and I devise and develop intelligent banking solutions to address challenging problems (e.g., Fraud Detection, Product Recommendation, Text Summarization, Sentiment Analysis, and Conversational Chatbots) based on state-of-the-art Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) algorithms.

Prior to CIBC, I completed my Ph.D. in Machine Learning at the University of Ottawa in Summer of 2018. I was nominated for, and subsequently won, Ontario Trillium Scholarship (OTS) - one of the prodigious scholarships in Ontario - when I started my Ph.D. in September 2014. For my Ph.D., I worked on Machine Learning, specifically on Adaptive and Continuous Learning from Massive Data Streams, and I published the results of my works in top-ranked venues including IJCNN 2018, ECML 2016, and Machine Learning Journal.

During my Ph.D., I independently learned about Deep Learning; particularly, I worked on Restricted Boltzmann Machines (RBMs) and Deep Autoencoders for semi-supervised learning in dynamic environments. I also had a collaboration with my brother, Ahmad Pesaranghader, for the Natural Language Processing (NLP) task of Word Sense Disambiguation (WSD) where we applied Bidirectional Long Short-Term Memory (BLSTM) network on top of concept embeddings. The results of our work will be published in Journal of the American Medical Informatics Association (JAMIA) of Oxford Academic.

I completed my Master's at the Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM), a top leading research university in Malaysia, back in 2013. During my Master's, I applied Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Theory concepts in designing semantic web spiders. The fruits of my thesis were conference papers and a book. I took my Bachelor's from the University of Kashan, an Iranian National University, in 2011. My Bachelor's thesis was about optimizing sensor networks, and I published it as a book later.

My current research interests are Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Information Retrieval. For more information, here are my Curriculum Vitae, Google Scholar, and LinkedIn.
